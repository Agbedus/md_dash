# Welcome to MD-Dash: Your Strategic Command Center 🚀

MD-Dash is a premium, AI-powered productivity platform designed for high-level management. It transforms raw data into mission-critical insights, ensuring you stay ahead of the curve with absolute data sovereignty.

---

## 🤖 The AI Aide: Your Intelligence Partner

Experience the future of productivity with our integrated AI Aide. Powered by state-of-the-art **local intelligence**, it acts as your personal Chief of Staff.

### ✨ Proactive Daily Briefings
Upon opening your dashboard, the AI Aide generates a natural language summary of your day. It analyzes your calendar, pending tasks, and urgent notifications to provide a briefing: *"Good morning. You have 3 critical meetings today. I recommend finalizing the 'Q3 Strategy' deck before your 11 AM session."*

### 🧩 Intelligent Task Decomposition
Turn "Mount Everest" goals into manageable steps. The AI Aide can automatically generate a detailed checklist of sub-tasks for complex objectives, saving you hours of manual planning.

### 💬 Interactive Intelligence
Chat directly with your data. Use the Assistant to "Show my high-priority tasks" or "Find my notes on the X project," and watch as interactive widgets bring that information to life instantly.

## 📊 The Command Center: Real-Time Analytics

The central hub provides a proactive, intelligent overview of your professional life, pushing actionable insights to help you start your day with clarity.

*   **Productivity Tracking**: Visualized trends of completed vs. pending work to measure your true output.
*   **Workload Distribution**: See exactly how work is spread across different projects and team members.
*   **Time Allocation**: A granular breakdown of where your time is spent (e.g., meetings vs. deep work).
*   **The "Top 3" Focus**: The AI suggests three items you should focus on today, providing a brief justification for each.

## 🎯 Operational Mastery: Tasks & Projects

Master your workflow with surgical precision. MD-Dash handles the cognitive load of project management so you can focus on execution.

### ⏱️ Integrated Time Tracking
Native timers are built into every task. Starting a timer automatically updates the task status and creates a `TimeLog` for accurate productivity analysis or billing.

### 🏗️ Project Pulse
Track your projects from planning to completion. Use the **Timeline (Gantt) View** to see overlapping initiatives and resource allocation at a glance.

### 🧠 Decision Synthesis
The **Decisions Hub** synthesizes notes, ideas, and documents into clear executive summaries. Turn brainstorming sessions into concrete operational decisions with ease.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security: The Local Advantage

Your data is your most sensitive asset. MD-Dash is built on a foundation of **Absolute Data Sovereignty**.

### 🏠 Local-AI Architecture
All AI capabilities are powered by industry-leading open-source models (Llama 3 / Mistral) running **locally** via Ollama. No sensitive data ever leaves your secure perimeter.

### 🔒 Role-Based Access (RBAC)
A robust security layer ensures data access is strictly controlled across Super Admins, Managers, Staff, and Clients.

### 🌐 Zero-Cloud Footprint
No telemetry. No cloud "training." Your strategy notes, meeting briefs, and financial tasks remain on your hardware, secured by JWT authentication.
